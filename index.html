Sports

One of extracurriculars includes sports. The sport I am in at school is Track & Field. My events include the 4x100 relay, 100m sprint, 4x400 relay, and long jump. Another sport I am a part of is S.R.L.A. (Students Run LA), a program that trains high school students to run the LA marathon for free!! To learn more click here. I completed the marathon of 2018. I personally believe that everyone should run one marathon in their life. Finishing the marathon was both the best (feeling accomplished) and the worst (the pain) feeling of my life.

Family

I come from a Thai background. My mom was born in the middle of Nebraska because my grandma was escaping the troubles she was facing in California. She went to UCLA where she got her biology degree. My dad was born in Thailand and came to America for college. He unfortunately did not finish college because his mom said she would not pay for it. My brother is on his way to M.I.T. this upcoming fall and is planning to major in material sciences. Both my mom and my brother inspire me. I definitely live in the shadow of my brother but hopefully not any longer.
